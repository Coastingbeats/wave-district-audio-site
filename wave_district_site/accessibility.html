Our Commitment

Accessibility at Wave District Audio

Wave District Audio is committed to making this website usable by as many people as possible, including people who use assistive technology such as screen readers, voice control, or keyboard-only navigation.

We work to follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA where practical. That includes things like descriptive alt text on images, labeled form fields, sufficient color contrast, a logical heading structure, and full keyboard navigability — including a "skip to content" link at the top of every page.

This is an ongoing effort. Websites and content change over time, and we review this site periodically to catch and fix accessibility issues as they come up.

Feedback

Run into a barrier?

If you have trouble accessing any part of this website, or need information in a different format, please let us know. Tell us the page you were on and what happened, and we'll work with you to get you the information or service you need — by phone, email, or another method that works for you.

This statement covers our website only. It doesn't apply to third-party sites we link to (like WeTransfer, Stripe, or Google), which maintain their own accessibility policies.
