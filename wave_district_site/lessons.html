Meet Your Instructor

Cameron Davis

Professionally trained in guitar, bass, and drums, with extensive touring experience. Cameron currently serves as Music Director at Animo Venice Charter High School, where he coached the program to three consecutive district-wide arts competition wins.

He's written, produced, and recorded multiple albums and EPs, and has built an audience of over 2 million views on TikTok performing original guitar pieces.

Cameron specializes in working with complete beginners and novices — including anyone who's tried to learn before and found it too difficult. No experience needed to get started.
