About Your Engineer

Cameron Davis

Raised on punk, death metal, noise, and the avant-garde, Cameron approaches recording with the belief that every band deserves to sound like themselves, not like everyone else. Strange microphone placements, loud amps bleeding into drum mics, unconventional rooms, live tracking, feedback, distortion, first takes, happy accidents — if it serves the song, it’s worth trying.

With more than ten years in Los Angeles’ DIY music community, Cameron has engineered projects spanning hardcore punk, crust, metal, goth, indie, experimental music, hyperpop, rap, and everything in between. The studio isn’t a place where ideas get shut down because they’re “wrong.” It’s a place to experiment, take risks, and discover what makes your project impossible to mistake for anyone else’s.

If you’ve ever been told, “That won’t work,” before anyone even gave it a shot, you’ll find a different experience here. Every session is built around capturing the sound that’s already in your head — not forcing your music through someone else’s process.
